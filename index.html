About me

G'day mate. I am an Australian living in Finland since 1989. Thanks to my Finnish wife, four adult children, and working in Humak I have fully adopted to Finnish culture, without forgetting my Aussie humor.

Most of the winter I spend in the cabin. I enjoy downhill skiing, during the 2018 to 2019 season I squeezed in 73 days of downhill. The next season was cut short because of the pandemic. I enjoy cross country skiing, snow shoe walking, hiking, travelling, cycling road and fat bike riding. Most summers I manage to ride over 1000 km. This place gives an enormous amount of outdoor hobbies. Photograhpy is also a hobby of mine. I enjoy taking pictures of the nature, people, my family. Especially taking pictures with the tripod of Northern lights is a great thrill. I find developing and making things very satisfying. I have built my own house and renovated a lot of things at the cabin. An Aussie has to have a garage in every house. Either being in software or welding something together with steel or making some with wood. My biggest metal project was a 10 tonne bogie axle forest trailer.
